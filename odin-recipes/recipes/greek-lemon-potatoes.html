Greek Lemon Potatoes

Roasted thick potato wedges are flavored with a garlic-lemon broth. This is one addictive dish!

Ingredients

  • 5 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Lemon, wedges
  • 1 tbsp Oregano, dried
  • 1 1/4 kg Potatoes

How do it?

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/390°F
  2. Cut potatoes
  3. Coat potatoes
  4. Roast 45 minutes
  5. To crisp the potatoes (optional)
  6. Roast 35 minutes
  7. Heat pan juices
  8. Plate up
